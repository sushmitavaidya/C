About Us

GOVERNMENT KANNADA HIGHER PRIMARY SCHOOL NO.5 TILAKWADI

was established in 1917 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Urban area. It is located in Belagavi CITY block of Belagavi district of Karnataka. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 8. Kannada is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in May.Children are provided with free uniform, textbook, meal, milk, shoes, socks, iron tablets. The school has Government building. It has got 8 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master. The school has have electric connection. It has 3 boys and 3 girls toilet which are maintained well. The school has playground. The school has a library and has 1315 books in its library. The school has a ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The food is Provided but not Prepared in School Premises providing mid-day meal.And there are 10 graduated teachers in school.About 250 students are studying in this school.
